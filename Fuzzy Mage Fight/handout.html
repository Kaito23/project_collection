Fuzzy form

First: draw a card for each of your minions

Actions:

  1. Draw a card
  2. Using fuzzy mage abilities
  3. Attack
  4. Place a card in your battlefield (max 6)
Essence Form

First: draw a card for each of your minions

Actions:

  1. Roll dice for 5 or 6 to return to fuzzy form
  2. Use essence abilities
  3. Fuze with other fuzzy mage in essence form
